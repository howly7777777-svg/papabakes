烘焙奶爸 Papa Bakes

繁體中文

本政策說明「烘焙奶爸」（Papa Bakes）app（以下稱「本 App」）如何處理您的資料。開發者：CHANG HAO LI。

設計原則：本機優先（Local-first）

不註冊帳號也能完整使用基礎與中級教學。收藏、個人筆記、語言偏好、離線下載的食譜，預設僅儲存在您的裝置上，不會自動上傳至我們的伺服器。

1. 我們收集的資料

  • 帳號（選用）：若您自願以電子郵件註冊／登入，我們透過 Supabase 儲存您的電子郵件與加密密碼，僅用於身分驗證。不註冊則無需提供電子郵件。
  • 裝置本機資料：收藏、個人筆記、語言設定、離線食譜快取——預設僅存於本機；刪除 App 即可清除。
  • 訂閱（Papa Bakes Plus）：付款由 Apple App Store 處理；我們不會取得您的信用卡號碼。我們僅能得知您是否擁有有效的訂閱，以解鎖高級食譜等進階內容。
  • 支援郵件：若您主動來信 howly7777777@gmail.com，我們會收到您提供的電子郵件地址與信件內容，僅用於回覆問題或建議。
  • v1 目前不收集：精確定位、通訊錄、相簿、公開社群貼文或留言（本 App 尚未提供社群功能）。

2. 資料用途

  • 提供食譜瀏覽、步驟教學、計時器與訂閱權益驗證
  • （選用）電子郵件帳號登入
  • 回覆您來信提出的支援請求

3. 第三方服務

  • Supabase：食譜內容資料庫；若您選擇註冊，亦用於帳號驗證
  • Apple：App 內購買與訂閱；適用 Apple 標準 EULA

4. 資料保存與刪除

來信要求刪除註冊帳號及相關雲端資料：howly7777777@gmail.com。本機資料請直接刪除 App。

5. 兒童隱私

本 App 以家庭烘焙為主題，適用年齡 4+。帳號註冊與訂閱應由成年人操作。我們不故意向 13 歲以下兒童收集個人資料。

6. 政策變更

若未來新增社群、雲端同步或作品上傳等功能，我們將更新本頁，並於 App Store 隱私標示中反映變更。

7. 聯絡我們

隱私、資料刪除或一般支援：
howly7777777@gmail.com

English

This policy describes how Papa Bakes ("the App") handles your information. Developer: CHANG HAO LI.

Design: Local-first

You can use basic and intermediate lessons without an account. Favorites, notes, language settings, and offline downloads are stored on your device by default and are not automatically uploaded to our servers.

1. Data we collect

  • Account (optional): If you choose to sign up or sign in with email, we store your email and hashed password via Supabase for authentication only. No email is required if you do not register.
  • On-device data: Favorites, notes, language preference, offline caches — stored locally; delete the App to remove them.
  • Subscriptions (Papa Bakes Plus): Processed by Apple. We do not receive your card number. We only learn whether you have an active subscription to unlock premium content.
  • Support email: If you contact howly7777777@gmail.com, we receive your address and message content to respond to your inquiry.
  • Not collected in v1: precise location, contacts, photo library, public community posts or comments (community features are not offered yet).

2. How we use data

  • Deliver recipes, step-by-step instructions, timers, and subscription entitlements
  • (Optional) Email sign-in
  • Respond to support requests

3. Third-party services

  • Supabase: recipe content database; authentication if you register
  • Apple: in-app purchases and subscriptions; Apple Standard EULA applies

4. Retention & deletion

Email us to delete a registered account: howly7777777@gmail.com. Remove local data by deleting the App.

5. Children's privacy

Rated 4+. Accounts and subscriptions should be managed by adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

6. Changes

If we add community, cloud sync, or photo uploads, we will update this page and App Store privacy labels.

7. Contact

Privacy, deletion, or support:
howly7777777@gmail.com
